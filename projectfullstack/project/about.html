About The Modern Frame

Discover our story, mission, and the passionate team behind our contemporary art gallery.

Our History

Founded in 1985 by renowned art collector Margaret Thornfield, The Modern Frame began as a small showcase for emerging contemporary artists. What started as a passion project in a converted warehouse has evolved into one of the city's most respected contemporary art venues.

Over nearly four decades, we have maintained our commitment to discovering and nurturing new talent while showcasing established masters of contemporary art. Our gallery has been instrumental in launching the careers of over 200 artists, many of whom have gone on to achieve international recognition.

"Art is not what you see, but what you make others see. At The Modern Frame, we strive to open new perspectives and challenge conventional thinking through the power of contemporary expression." - Margaret Thornfield, Founder

Notable Artists

Throughout our history, we have been privileged to work with exceptional artists who have shaped the contemporary art landscape:

  • Sarah Mitchell - Abstract Expressionist known for urban-inspired works
  • James Chen - Minimalist sculptor working primarily in stone and metal
  • Maria Rodriguez - Geometric painter exploring mathematical beauty
  • David Kim - Contemporary sculptor specializing in dynamic forms
  • Alex Thompson - Digital artist pioneering cyberpunk aesthetics
  • Elena Volkov - Photographer capturing urban solitude
  • Roberto Santos - Mixed media artist celebrating cultural diversity
  • Sophie Laurent - Expressionist painter of emotional landscapes
