Plan Your Visit

Admission

Free admission to all exhibitions. Guided tours available for $15 per person with advance booking.

Accessibility

Our gallery is fully wheelchair accessible with ramps and accessible restrooms. Audio tours are available for visually impaired visitors.

Photography

Personal photography is welcome in most areas. Flash photography and tripods are not permitted. Some special exhibitions may have restrictions.
